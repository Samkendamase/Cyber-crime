If your email an password are available to the public, people can make online purchases 
A person can even sign you out of your own email 
They can pretend to be you and send out emails to your work place or pretend to be you on your other online platforms

To protect yourself against data breach one should you a passord that is not too obvious, use a strong password and sign up for a two factor authentication
Avoid casually filling in your details on any link that you may see online or on your emails (Garfinkel et al 2005)

Reference 
Garfinkel, S.L., Margrave, D., Schiller,J.I., Nordlander, E. and Miller, R.C., 2005, April. How to make secure email easier to use. In Proceedings of the SIGCHI conference on human factors in computing systems (pp. 701-710).
